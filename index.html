What is Catalyst?

IIT Mandi Catalyst offers a low-cost, peaceful and picturesque destination to early stage startups. Catalyst, a Technology Business Incubator (TBI), in town Mandi in Himachal Pradesh aims to support budding entrepreneurs who intend to develop innovations that would shape the world of tomorrow. Through its support programs, Catalyst nurtures and guides early stage entrepreneurial initiatives by facilitating wide range of support that includes infrastructure, finances, mentoring, and industry connections.

Launched in 2016, IIT Mandi Catalyst is the first Technology Business Incubator (TBI) in the state of Himachal Pradesh. Catalyst has its separate legal entity as a Section 8 Not for profit entity registered with Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

How is Catalyst supported?

Catalyst was awarded INR 4.5 crores funding by Department of Science and Technology, GoI, for the period 2016-21. IIT Mandi has committed about INR 8 crores for the same duration. In addition, Vishal Bharat Comnet has donated INR 10 Lakhs toward incubation funds of IIT Mandi Catalyst. In 2017, Government of Himachal Pradesh committed INR 30 Lakhs for Catalyst.

Tedx IIT Mandi

Venue: South Campus, IIT Mandi
Event date: 14th May 2017
Participants: IIT Mandi Students

Himalayan Startup Trek

Venue: South Campus, IIT Mandi
Date: 8th & 9th July 2017
Participants: Early stage startups, Entrepreneurs, Speakers, Investors & IIT Mandi Students.
Event Details: IIT Mandi Catalyst hosted the first edition of 'Himalayan Startup Trek'. It was an effort to boost the ecosystem of startups in the state. Catalyst invited early stage startups with technology based business ideas from Pan-Himalayan region to attend the event. This event featured a two day boot camp at IIT Mandi Campus which also included a pitching competition winners of which were offered a place in incubation/exploration program at Catalyst.

Venue: Co- working space
Speaker: Vishwanathan S, Co – founder & VP, FORGE Innovation Accelerator
Date: 4th & 5th October 2017
Participants: IIT Mandi Catalyst startup teams
Session Details: A two-day workshop on MVB Boot Camp methodology in which the mentor shared the rationale behind the MVB framework. Startup teams went through the MVB Boot Camp methodology and refined their value propositions and business models.

Mentor Series: Product Design

Venue: South Campus, IIT Mandi
Speaker: Mr. Aakash Kumar, Associate Director, Make My Trip
Date: 14th & 15th October
Participants: IIT Mandi Catalyst startup teams

Mentor Series: Startup Legal

Venue: South Campus, IIT Mandi
Speaker: Mr. Aditya Shamlal, Partner, Gamechanger Law Advisor
Date: 26th October 2017
Participants: IIT Mandi Catalyst startup teams, IIT Mandi Faculty members and students.
Session Details: Startup teams learnt about incorporating a company, technology contracts, corporate contracts and fundraising transactions. Aditya had advised multiple start-ups and SMEs on their fundraising transactions, commercial transactions and contracts, incorporation, terms of service, privacy policy, employment agreements, exchange management related requirements and much more. It was a good opportunity for startup teams , faculty members and research Scholars to understand the legal process of starting their own company, commercializing technologies and corporate engagement.

Mentor Series: Patents & IP

Venue: South Campus, IIT Mandi
